Different types of Momos

True taste of Tibet

Steamed Momo

The basic type of momo is stream momo. It comes in different favors/meat i.e, chicken, beef etc. Each plate of momo accompany with delicious chilli sauce.

Fried Momo

Fried Momos are simply fried steamed momo. These momos are crispy outside and juicy inside.

Kurkure Momos

There are momos that have the coating of the famous Lehar Kurkure over a juicy filling.

Tandoori Momos

There are momos that have the coating of the Tandoori coating with tandoori chicken as filling.

Afghani Momos

Afghani momos prepared and marinated in a creamier batter. The momos are first marinated and then roasted in the tandoor just like the tandoori momos.

Kothey Momos

pan-fried momos with different types of sauces depending upon what you choose. There are different sauces available for you to pick from.

Chocolate Momos

This momo is sweet and filled with chocolate.
